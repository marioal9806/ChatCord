Welcome To ChatCord!

ChatCord is a free app that lets you share your ideas on different topics.

You can join different Chat Rooms and interact with the connected users.

It's easy to start!
By just using a nickname you can start chatting.
If you prefer, you can also create an account to store your messages and preferences.
